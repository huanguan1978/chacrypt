## Our Privacy-First Utility Ecosystem

All our tools are designed with a commitment to being cross-platform, ad-free, registration-free, pure, and free to use. We prioritize your privacy above all else, ensuring a professional and completely free experience.

### Privacy & Encryption Tools
- **ChaBox**: An all-in-one privacy-focused toolset for secure file management. [GitHub](https://github.com/huanguan1978/chacrypt/tree/main/chabox)
- **Chapose**: A professional CLI utility for secure and robust cryptographic operations. [GitHub](https://github.com/huanguan1978/chacrypt/tree/main/chapose)

### File Management & Automation
- **ft (filetools)**: A high-performance CLI for advanced file management. Features precise glob filtering, multi-constraint secondary filtering (size/time/mime), a robust suite of built-in subcommands, and seamless shell/YAML orchestration for scalable automation. [GitHub](https://github.com/huanguan1978/ft)
- **FileShows**: The visual companion to `ft`. Beyond intuitive GUI operations and visual batch processing, it supports automated scheduling for your custom YAML command sets and comprehensive integrated execution log management. [Web](https://webpath.iche2.com/fssdoc/en/)

### Beginner-Friendly Generative AI Toolkit
- **Gai**: Generative-AI tools for beginners. By providing a clean, non-prescriptive foundation, full-parameter control, and comprehensive built-in documentation, Gai enables you to master core AI technologies through hands-on experience, allowing AI to truly serve your daily life and work. [Web](https://webpath.iche2.com/gaidoc/en/)
- **PyGai**: The powerful Python-based automation bridge for `Gai`, enabling batch prompt injection, intelligent content scheduling, and seamless AI workflow integration. [GitHub](https://github.com/huanguan1978/pygai)
